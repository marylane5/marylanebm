About Me

Hi, I’m Mary Beem — a web and digital design student focused on marketing, user experience, and simple, clean designs. I love mixing design and marketing to make websites that actually work for people. I’ve built responsive sites, UX/UI prototypes, JavaScript projects, and full social media + email campaigns.

I’m also a mom and military wife who loves traveling, exploring new ideas, and testing creative apps. I enjoy learning new skills and creating work that feels meaningful and clear.

Career Goal: Build a digital marketing career where I can mix creativity, accessibility, user-friendly design, and smart strategies. I’m especially interested in UX research, mobile-first design, analytics, and modern web tools.
